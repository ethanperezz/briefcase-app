# Briefcase — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Target Customer
Solo freelancers and consultants (designers, developers, copywriters, marketers) managing 2-15 clients who currently use email + Google Drive + Venmo/PayPal.

## Positioning
"The anti-HoneyBook" — simple, affordable, does one thing well.

## Launch Channels (in order of priority)

### Week 1: Organic Launch
1. **Product Hunt** — Submit with screenshots, demo GIF, and compelling tagline
2. **Indie Hackers** — Post in Show section with business metrics focus
3. **Hacker News** — Show HN with technical angle (open source, minimal stack)
4. **Reddit** — r/SideProject, r/freelance, r/webdev, r/Entrepreneur
5. **Twitter/X** — Launch thread, quote-tweet from supporters

### Week 2-4: Content Marketing
1. **Blog posts**: "Why I Stopped Using Email to Manage Clients"
2. **Comparison pages**: Briefcase vs HoneyBook, Briefcase vs Dubsado
3. **Tutorial**: "How to Set Up a Client Portal in 2 Minutes"
4. **YouTube**: Quick demo video (screen recording)

### Month 2: Community
1. Join freelancer Facebook groups, Slack communities, Discord servers
2. Answer "how do you manage clients?" questions on Reddit/Quora
3. Partner with freelancer newsletters for sponsored mentions
4. Reach out to freelance YouTubers for reviews

## Pricing Strategy
- **Free tier** (2 clients) — reduces friction, gets word of mouth
- **Pro at $19/mo** — below the pain threshold, annual discount to $15/mo
- No free trial timer — free tier IS the trial

## Key Metrics to Track
- Signups per week
- Free-to-paid conversion rate (target: 5-10%)
- Portal links shared (virality indicator)
- Client messages sent (engagement)
- MRR

## Revenue Targets
- Month 1: 50 signups, 5 paying = $95 MRR
- Month 3: 200 signups, 30 paying = $570 MRR
- Month 6: 500 signups, 75 paying = $1,425 MRR
- Month 12: 2,000 signups, 300 paying = $5,700 MRR

## Competition
| Tool | Price | Target | Why we win |
|------|-------|--------|-----------|
| HoneyBook | $40/mo | Agencies | Simpler, cheaper, faster setup |
| Dubsado | $25/mo | Agencies | Less bloated, no learning curve |
| Notion | Free | Everyone | Purpose-built for client portals |
| Email | Free | Everyone | Organized, professional, branded |

## Differentiation
1. **No client login** — share a link, they're in
2. **Branded** — shows YOUR business name, not ours
3. **Simple** — takes 2 min to set up, not 2 hours
4. **Affordable** — $19/mo, not $40-80/mo
5. **Open source** — self-host for free if you want
