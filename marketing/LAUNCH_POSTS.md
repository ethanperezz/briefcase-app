# Briefcase — Launch Marketing Posts

## Product Hunt Tagline
**Client portals for freelancers — set up in 2 minutes, not 2 hours**

## Product Hunt Description

### The Problem
Freelancers juggle Gmail threads, Google Drive links, Venmo requests, and Slack messages across every client. Your client asks "what's the status?" and you spend 10 minutes digging through emails to give them an update. Sound familiar?

### The Solution
Briefcase gives every client their own branded portal — one link where they can see project progress, download deliverables, message you, and view invoices.

**No login required for clients.** You share a link. They're in.

### How It Works
1. Sign up and add your clients
2. Create projects with milestones
3. Share the portal link with your client
4. Your client sees: progress, files, invoices, and can message you — all in one place

### Built for solo freelancers, not agencies
Existing tools like HoneyBook ($40/mo) and Dubsado ($25/mo) are bloated with features you don't need. Briefcase does one thing well: give your clients a professional portal experience.

**$19/mo. Unlimited clients. Unlimited projects.**
